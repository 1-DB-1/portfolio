Learn UX skills on the go

OVERVIEW

Micro moments are short bursts of time that consumers spend on a technological device, usually a smartphone. These moments can happen anywhere, at any time. Some common examples include waiting for a friend or public transport to arrive and during a break at work. Learning on the go is becoming more and more common, this application is a great example for that.

THE GOAL

One lesson daily
Lesson disappear in 24 hour
All 7 lesson from previous week can be followed after 1 week
Learning is through the processing of Case Studies
It has built-in gemification in the application. Badges, motivation for users to pass 1 lesson per day
User profile with badges and finnished lessons.
